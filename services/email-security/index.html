Why Email Remains the #1 Attack Vector

Despite being decades old, email remains the single most exploited communication channel by threat actors. Sophisticated spear-phishing, CEO fraud (BEC), and malware-laden attachments bypass native platform filters daily. Twiis applies multi-layered AI analysis — combining sandboxing, link rewriting, sender verification, and behavioral anomaly detection — to stop threats that Microsoft Defender and Google's built-in filters miss.
