Overview

Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT) is the cornerstone of any mature security program. Our AI-Guided VAPT combines automated reconnaissance, exploit simulation, and lateral movement testing with expert human analysis. We simulate real-world adversary tactics, techniques, and procedures (TTPs) to expose exploitable weaknesses across web applications, APIs, internal networks, and cloud infrastructure — then deliver actionable remediation roadmaps.
