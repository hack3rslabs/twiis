Overview

Every device on your network is a potential entry point for adversaries. Our Intelligent Endpoint Defense solution deploys behavioral AI models that continuously monitor process activity, file system changes, and network connections across all endpoints. Unlike legacy signature-based antivirus, our system detects zero-day exploits and fileless malware in real time — stopping threats before they can propagate across your environment.
