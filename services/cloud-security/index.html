Overview

Cloud misconfigurations are responsible for the majority of cloud-related data breaches. Our CSPM solution provides continuous, automated scanning of your cloud environment to identify exposed storage buckets, overpermissioned IAM roles, unencrypted databases, and compliance drift across AWS, Azure, and GCP. Our AI engine maps risks to regulatory frameworks in real time, giving your team actionable remediation guidance instantly.
