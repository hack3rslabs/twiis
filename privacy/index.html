Privacy Policy

Your Data, Our Priority. Last updated: April 2026.

1. Introduction

Twiis Innovations OPC Pvt. Ltd. ("Twiis", "we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website twiis.in or engage with our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and corporate email address (via contact forms)
  • Company name and service requirements
  • Technical information submitted during security engagements

We also automatically collect certain technical information such as IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and time spent — used solely for site analytics and security purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide requested services
  • To send relevant security updates or service communications (with consent)
  • To improve our website experience and service offerings
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our legal rights

4. Data Protection Standards

We adhere to GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act) standards. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256 encryption. We do not sell your personal data to third parties under any circumstances.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. Contact inquiry data is retained for a maximum of 24 months unless a contractual relationship is established.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable data protection laws, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at help@twiis.in.

7. Cookies

Our website uses essential session cookies only. We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies that profile individual users.

8. Third-Party Services

We may use limited third-party services (such as CDN providers for font and icon delivery). These services have their own privacy policies and are selected for their strong data protection practices.

9. Contact Us

For any privacy-related queries, please contact our Data Protection Officer at: help@twiis.in
